DFD COMPONENTS:
{dfd_json}

YOUR TASK:
For each significant PROCESS in the DFD, produce:

A) ACTIVATION TABLE
   - Lists the CONDITION (event or input) that activates each process.
   - Format: one row per activation condition per process.

B) DECISION TABLE
   - For processes that contain branching logic, define:
     * Conditions (the IF part — what inputs or states are evaluated)
     * Actions    (the THEN part — what the process does)
     * Rules      (each column = one combination of condition values → actions)

RULES:
- Cover ALL processes from the DFD.
- Use Y (yes/true), N (no/false), or – (irrelevant/don't-care) for condition values.
- Use X to mark which actions fire under each rule.
- Keep condition and action labels short (3–6 words).
